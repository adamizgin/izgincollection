Frequently asked questions


How do I order a product?

Orders are made via Instagram's Direct Message to our official Instagram account @izgin.collection or send a private message to adam.izgin on FaceBook. Tell us which product you would like to order (plus eventual additions) and where to send the product.
An example of an order would be:

– Hi! I'd like to purchase the Lord's prayer in acrylic aluminium glass, 30×45cm with an invisible wall mount.



Which payment method is acceptable?

PayPal, or Swish if you live in Sweden.



When will my product be delivered?

It usually takes 9 business days for the products to be delivered. But it can be different depending on in which country you live.



How much does the shipping cost?

The shipping is free if you live in Sweden. If you live inside the EU, the shipping amount will be from 15€ to 20€. Shipping outside of the EU is currently not possible.
